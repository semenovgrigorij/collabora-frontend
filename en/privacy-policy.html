Privacy Policy of the Collabora platform

Introduction

We respect your right to privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and business data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, the purposes for which we use it, the measures we take to safeguard it, and your rights regarding your information.

Data You Provide:

We only collect open (non-confidential) information, including:

  • Full name and job title
  • Company name, field of activity, and location
  • Contact details (email, phone number)
  • Information submitted through registration forms and applications
  • Platform usage data (including cookies)

Who Has Access to Your Data:

Your data is processed exclusively by the automated collaboration matching system (AI module).

The platform administration has limited access solely for technical support purposes.

Other platform participants can view your data only with your explicit consent.

How We Protect Your Information:

All data is transmitted via secure SSL encryption.

Access to databases is restricted to authorized technical personnel only.

We conduct regular security audits to ensure the safety and integrity of our systems.

Cookies and Analytics

The platform uses cookies to improve user experience, collect statistics, and analyze user activity. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

Each user has the right to:

  • View, modify, or delete their data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request complete deletion of their account

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy periodically. All changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
