Our Mission

Collabora was born in a time of trials — as a response to the challenges that Ukrainian entrepreneurs, manufacturers, creators, and innovators face every day.

We are a team that has gone through the path of losses and recovery. That's why we created a platform that unites Ukrainian business and helps find partners faster, simpler, and more accurately — thanks to the power of artificial intelligence.

Our mission — to strengthen business through interaction, to create an environment where entrepreneurs don't compete, but collaborate. Where even a small manufacturer can find a big partner, and a creative team — their large-scale project.

Collabora is not just a platform. It's a space of trust, development, and solutions. A space where business comes alive through collaboration.

Social Collaboration

We believe that business should not only generate profit, but also create value for society.

That's why Collabora is also about people.

The platform unites entrepreneurs who seek not only profit, but also meaning. We support:

Local initiatives and craft manufacturing

Entrepreneurs who lost their business due to war

Women entrepreneurs, veterans, internally displaced persons

Social projects that change communities

Volunteer initiatives that help the army, hospitals, children

Businesses implementing support programs during wartime

Our AI system sees not only matches, but also the potential for shared impact.

We strive for collaboration on Collabora to become a driver of positive change in the country.
